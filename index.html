Context
Why Vegas Plumbing
Fails So Often

Extreme Hard Water

Vegas water runs 15-24 GPG (Very High). This mineral buildup destroys standard water heaters in 6-8 years instead of 12.

Slab Foundations

Most Vegas homes are built on concrete slabs. When pipes under the slab leak, they require specialized detection equipment to locate.

Thermal Expansion

115°F summers cause pipes to expand and contract aggressively, leading to joint failures and sudden bursts.
