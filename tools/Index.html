Vegas Water Rate Impact

Las Vegas homeowners are subject to the LVVWD 2024 Tiered Rate Schedule. Unlike other cities, Vegas rates are designed to penalize high volume usage to encourage conservation.

A "silent leak" like a running toilet can easily add 3,000 to 5,000 gallons per month to your bill. This volume doesn't just cost more; it pushes your entire bill into Tier 3 or Tier 4, where the cost per gallon nearly quadruples.

Current LVVWD Residential Tiers

  • Tier 1 (Essential): $1.45 per 1,000 Gal
  • Tier 2 (Standard): $2.58 per 1,000 Gal
  • Tier 3 (Elevated): $3.92 per 1,000 Gal
  • Tier 4 (Excessive): $5.61 per 1,000 Gal

Hard Water & Leaks

The high mineral content in Vegas water (Hard Water) accelerates the degradation of rubber flappers and fixture seals. This leads to slow drips that go unnoticed but cause massive "Financial Drain."

If your bill has increased by more than 20% without a change in habits, you likely have a volumetric leak in your primary line or irrigation system.
